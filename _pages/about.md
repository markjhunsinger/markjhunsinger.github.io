### Accomplished software engineer, determined problem solver, Tarzan wannabe.

I like to figure things out. I enjoy taking things apart and putting them back together.

I am equal parts programming obsessed and IT savvy, constantly upgrading my skill set and fine tuning my programming skills through learning software and real-world experience.

My ultimate goal is to obtain a career in software engineering. I have an interest in app and game development for PC and mobile devices, as well as an enthusiasm for 3D modeling and cryptocurrencies.

I am a parkour coach, where I live by [Georges Hébert](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Georges_H%C3%A9bert)'s motto - "Être fort pour être utile" ("Being strong to be useful"). I find that maintaining this mindset allows me to become a better person both physically and mentally and allows me to overcome any challenge that crosses my path.

I am extremely excited about the future of programming and technology. I believe that the future is code, and I am excited to see what's in store for humanity in the world of technology and computer sciences.
